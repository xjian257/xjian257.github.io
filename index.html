Bio

Xiaohan is the last year PhD student in Structural & HRR Program at Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at Western University, supervised by Dr. Hanping Hong, Dr. Wenxing Zhou and Dr. Jiandong Ren. His research focuses on the following fields:

  • Simulation of nonstationly and non-Gaussian process.

  • Assessment of regional seismic loss.

I am glad to discuss ideas about my research interests, so please feel free to reach out!

  • Jan. 2025: Our paper "Simulating multi-component near-fault ground motion record containing pulse-like component" has been accepted by Soil Dynamics and Earthquake Engineering (IF:4.6).
